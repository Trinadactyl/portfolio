Bio

I grew up around the world, and have always been well accustomed to cultural diversity and rapid change! As soon as I was introduced to the global tech community I was immediately hooked. I saw not only an opportunity to contribute to some of the world's leading innovations, I also saw a clear path to creative and geographical freedom. Next to coding, I am also an enthusiastic photographer and music junkie. I can be found at my local concert venue, just inches from the stage, firing away on my Canon 77D. Or hiking the trails of a State park, or natural reserve, camera-ready.
